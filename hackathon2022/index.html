Welcome to #BBallATL

#BBallATL Community Mission serves to instill sportsmanship & ethics in our local youth. Our BBallATL app network works together through competitive BBall statistics to leverage our community assets, parks & free basketball courts; The BBallATL Community connects our local youth to a local walkable city park with a secure court to encourage constructive, leadership focused recreation together which also creates community harmony and local impacts from NBA and Civic sponsorships.
